The First Day in London

Posted on 2019-04-06

(Sorry for the Chinese-British mix. It is the first version and I will update it when I return)

start off

早上6:30起来打包昨天剩下的行李，Q2接到我，一起去艺园吃了油条豆浆，8:20之后搭乘出租车前往T3

出租路上堵车，大概9:40左右才到T3，前一晚上已经选了座位，直接找到托运处很快就办完。然后就去登机，现在是电子化可以走自动口，分别刷一下护照和登机牌就可以识别信息，不得不说实在是方便太多。之后就是搭摆渡车到国际登机站台，开始安检，因为行李都已经把电子产品拆分出来，也没有带液体化妆品，所以整个过程也很快。然后就开始悠哉悠哉逛免税店啦。有一种觉得很划算又不知道买啥的错觉，和波波建的群有人陆陆续续发图，基本上在原价基础上收0.1，所以也算进账一二百，这算是第一次尝试自己赚些小钱，之后会更有经验一些。

现在坐在了飞机上。马上就要开始自己的英国游，着实有些不真切。希望这趟旅行之后的自己变得更open-minded！

拥挤的经济舱～

Arrivals

I almost arrived at 4:15 pm, but it took a little bit of time to queue up to get off the plane.

When I inserted the local card in my phone and connected it to the Internet, I find a lot of wechat messages, and I have to reply some of them immediately. Especially Prof.Ma asked me and my senior fellow sister to recruit a RA to assist the Prof.Deng in UCl.

Then I went the luggage carousel to pick up mine, however, unfortunately I found my suitcase was damaged with a wheel missing. I felt shocked and didn’t know what they did to my luggage in the flight. I am so inexperienced that I decided to meet my friend Bo first and dealt with these things latter. So I went into the exit passage and missed the luggage enquiries room which it’s a no-entry way, means I can’t go back again.

It is so excited to meet my Bo after three months and she gave me a warm bear hug the instant she saw me! She also bring me some delicious cookies which is a peanuts flavor, her favorite. And she immediately found there is something wrong with my luggage and urged me to the consult desk to solve this problem, not leave the airport terminal in case the airport won’t take responsibility for this. We consulted some airport stuffs and went to the luggage enquiries to make a phone call. But the stuff on the other side of the phone said we had to claim compensation online, because we can’t entered into the luggage carousel spot since it’s a no-entry way.

We filled the table in the ba.com, but I thought there is little possibility of reclaiming the compensation for damage.

落地取了行李，却发现少了一个轮子，想到出来见了波波再去机场处咨询解决，但是后来打luggage enquiries电话，对方说因为我们已经出了出口，不能再进去了，所以现在只能online填申请处理。bad luck

The journey begins!

Bo opened my luggage and took the Chinese food I brought from Beijing supermarket. She said it is so delicious and felt so content.

London is almost 8 hours behind Beijing, so it is almost midnight in Beijjng. Although I have took enough sleep in the plane, I still felt a little jetlag reactions.

We also took sometime to find the right way to the subway. Bo said the system is so confusing and she got lost this morning. As for the luggage things, we have no time to see the British Museum in the evening and we decided to take the subway from the Heathrow terminal 5 to the king’s cross station directly and walk to our hostel click78, which is only ten minutes’ walk.

The surroundings around is surprising to me. When we got off the subway and saw the classical station with an ancient outlook but modern equipments inside. I can’t help to take some photos but I don’t think I may catch the true beauty.

然后我们搭乘subway从Heathrow Terminal 5到King’s Cross station，去参观了9 3/4 station, the stuffs here will assist you how to take a professional photograph with the scarf I the house you love.
Then we went to the souvenir shop. I was so surprised the moment I walked into it.看了纪念品store，就回我们住的click78，波带了cookies和sushi给我，路过超市买了whole milk全脂奶，晚上也在hostel bar喝了lemonade.
We talked to some people at the dining room. I find Bo talented in making friends and it seems a little hard for me to get understood and express my points.

Knowledge Time

今天跟波学到了
可以用cheers代替thanks
别人说thanks，你可以说no worries
would you like sth？or do you like sth？ I am fine or I am ok就是不用了的意思
只想听别人说，就可以说Cool
觉得某人做的好，就可以说Good for you
whole/skimmed/semi-skimmed milk
跟别人讲话一定要专心，如果不是特别好，可以说i am learning my English，听不懂可以问出来。
做饭带给别人，可以问 are u a vegetarian or vegan？
